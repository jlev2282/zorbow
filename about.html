Zorbow was created in 1989 by Vincent Fitzgerald, the founder of Abby Educational Products. Mr. Fitzgerald designed Zorbow as a way to eleviate the stress that some children experience in learning Math, but turning the learning process into a game. His hope was that through the spirit of competition, children who struggled with Math would grow confident in their abilities. I was one of those children that played one of the earliest versions of Zorbow. Mr. Fitzgerald and his game not only helped me see that learning can be fun, but that there are many ways to learn. This version of Zorbow is an update to the original while still embodying the structure and intent of the game. The product is free to use. Enjoy!
